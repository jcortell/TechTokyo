TECH-Tokyo

The Technology & Entrepreneurship Center at Harvard Tokyo Hub program (TECH-Tokyo) is an initiative of the Technology & Entrepreneurship Center at Harvard's John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (TECH-SEAS) founded and led by its Senior International Innovation Fellow, Jorge Cortell, in collaboration with Harvard University Office of Technology Development, the Harvard University Innovation Laboratories, and several Harvard University Executive Education units.

OBJECTIVE

Bringing Harvard University faculty, researchers, and entrepreneurs, and Japanese corporations, entrepreneurs, investors, and universities, closer together to explore academic, partnering, and commercial opportunities through a multi-year program of presentations, lectures, workshops, networking, guest speakers, competitions and more.

IN SHIBUYA-TOKYO

Attend lectures and presentations with simultaneous translation in Japanese-English in Shibuya-Tokyo Tokyo, and collaborate in real time with Harvard students, entrepreneurs, and researchers using a full-size real-time video portal.
